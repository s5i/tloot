Instructions:
  1. Take a partial screenshot (Win + Shift + S) of the loot.
    • See example below.
    • Important: the screenshot needs to be in the original scale and quality.
  2. (optional) Disable items you don't care about for faster processing.
    • Note: this doesn't affect existing results.
  3. (optional) Adjust prices.
    • Yellow background → market price (NPC price is missing or terrible).
    • Bold font → price was modified.
  4. Hit Ctrl + V.
Issues?
  • Known issues:
    • WASM script sometimes fails to load on Opera and Firefox. Try reloading.
    • Items known to be misdetected are force-disabled.
  • Issue not listed above? Contact Shy Teddy (shyym on Discord).
    • If DevTools console (F12 on most browsers) errors are present, attach those.
    • Wrong item count? Attach the screenshot you used.
